FR HandyMan Training & Service Centre

Introduction

Our training center has established in Jan 2016. Mr Chau Kam Choong, the founder of centre, has over 20 years experiences in the household air-conditioner industry. He is an expert who also specialized in other household services, such as electrical wiring, water plumbing, household appliances maintenaince and others. The purpose why he founded this centre is to give people an opportunity to learn useful handy skills in order to survive in this society.

Courses

We provide different level of courses, that are Junior, Intermediate and Senior courses. The details of courses can be found below.

Junior : 60% Practical, 40% Theory
  • Briefing for General Knowledge & Common Troubleshooting Skill
  • Safety Knowledge for HandyMan, Marketing Strategy & Knowledge
  • *Air-Conditioner Repair & Maintenance
  • *Water Heater Repair & Maintenance
  • *Refrigerator Repair & Maintenance
  • *Auto-Gate Repair & Maintenace
  • *Washing Machine Repair & Maintenance
  • *House Wiring Repair & Maintenace
  • *Water Plumbing Repair & Maintenance
  * Basic knowledge applied

Intermediate : 50% Practical, 50% Theory
  • *Air-Conditioner Repair & Maintenance
  • *Water Heater Repair & Maintenance
  • *Refrigerator Repair & Maintenance
  • *Auto-Gate Repair & Maintenace
  • *Washing Machine Repair & Maintenance
  • *House Wiring Repair & Maintenace

Requirements
